A House Divided: The Walking Dead continues

09 March 2014 on The Walking Dead

Episode 2 of season 2 of Telltale Game's The Walking Dead was just released last week, and I finally had a chance to play it. I won't go into it in-depth like I did the last episode, partly because the previous episode established much of what occurred in this episode, and partly because much of my thoughts on that episode apply to this one as well.

There wasn't as much "new" in this episode, but that is actually a good thing. In retrospect, I realize that the previous episode was actually a little stilted due to having to introduce so much so fast, and some of the characters came off a little shallow. Not so in this episode. Telltale knocked this one out of the park, and it is, in my opinion, one of the best episodes of both seasons.
