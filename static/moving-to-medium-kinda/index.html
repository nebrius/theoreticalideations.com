Moving to Medium, Kinda

13 February 2015

You may have noticed that things have been kinda quite around here lately. Well, I've moved to Medium for my blogging. This is mostly because I can really increase my reach there compared to my own self-hosted blog.

That said, I have a lot of content here that I'm rather fond of, so I'm going to keep this site up in parallel. I will be cross-posting everything from Medium to here going forward. Admittedly I have posted a handful already and forgot to cross-post, but I'll be good about it going forward.

You can check out my Medium blog at https://medium.com/@nebrius/!
